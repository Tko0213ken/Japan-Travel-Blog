DISCOVER JAPAN

Hello! I am Mariko, a travel enthusiast born and raised in Japan. Currently living in Tokyo, I enjoy capturing local cuisine and beautiful natural scenery across Japan.
On this blog, I share recommended travel destinations and delicious restaurants that only someone with my local knowledge can provide.
I hope to convey Japan's rich culture and charm to you. Enjoy your journey with us!
